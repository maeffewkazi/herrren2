Established 2021

Founded because someone had to break the rules.

(Herren)²® is a design studio based in Switzerland—working internationally to design iconic brands and digital experiences with focus on what’s essential.

Founding partners Matthew + Petra Herren. Plus their kin. Zürich, 2021.

 The world is more dynamic and complex than ever. The speed with which new ideas are born, developed and realised is unlike any other period of time, resulting in a positive optimism to define what’s next.

Speed and agility permeate the business environment. From startups to big corporations, the desire to innovate is ever present. Transformation is a constant.

But rapid progression shouldn’t come at the expense of craft and consideration. That’s why (Herren)²® exists. Our ambition is to create work that outlasts tomorrow and supports people in their everyday lives. As designers our responsibility is to use thoughtful design to help shape a more meaningful, no-nonsense and intelligent future.

It starts with understanding and truly caring for the essentials.

What is the real problem at play? Are we considering the longevity of the solution? Could we improve rather than add? Could we simplify rather than complicate? Good work takes time, commitment and close collaboration. We value long-lasting relationships where trust, openness and progress become the driver for the process. A shared set of values is essential.

The studio was born in 2021 with an ambition to create order in the increasingly more complex convergence between business and design. Based in Switzerland we work with ambitious corporations, startups and individuals all over the world.

(Herren)²®® is founded and led by Matthew Herren, who taught himself design and development growing up in Africa in the 80s and 90s. Always a dreamer, head in the clouds, his wife Petra keeps the train on the tracks.

Founders, the Herren's squared + their followers.

Process

Services

We help brands imagine and test the future of their business potential; create unique design languages; and launch new products and services into the world. Independent of the output, we strive to offer new perspectives on established assumptions, methods and solutions.
